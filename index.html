Like me, do you love to shop especially when there is a sale going on ? Do you find yourself googling for deals or ouring over newspaper to see if your favorite store has a sale going on ? Do you have friends who find the most amazing bargains ? Don't you hate it when after paying you come to know that the things you just purchased would have been 10% cheaper if you had used you so and so credit card. If one or more of this is true, then I have the perfect app for you. Your own shopping assistant, SpotADeal.

Every morning, SpotADeal will have the latest and greatest deals summarized for you. You enter a shopping mall and it will find the stores you could visit and places you could eat. Looking for something specific, ask SpotADeal and it will help you find it. You found a great steal, SpotADeal it to your friends and they will do the same for you.

What is this about ?
I am taking a course on Startup Engineering @ Coursera. This product is my class project. The intent of the course is to enable students to float their own startups. However, I would recommend this course for all software engineers. It introduces you all tools and utilities you should know about but did not bother to learn about.
